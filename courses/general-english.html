Course Modules

Module 1: English Foundations

Basic sentence structure, present tenses, common vocabulary, pronunciation basics, and simple conversations with practice activities.

Module 2: Everyday Communication

Introducing yourself, asking and answering questions, daily routines, shopping and travel English, and role-play practice sessions.

Module 3: Grammar & Structure

Past and future tenses, modal verbs, prepositions, conditionals, and grammar practice tests for mastery.

Module 4: Reading & Listening

Short text reading, listening to conversations, understanding main ideas, note-taking techniques, and comprehension exercises.

Module 5: Speaking & Pronunciation

Fluency development, accent reduction, natural intonation, discussion techniques, and presentation practice.

Module 6: Real-Life Application

Job interviews, social situations, phone conversations, email writing, and final speaking assessment.
