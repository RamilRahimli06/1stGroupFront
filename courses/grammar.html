Course Modules

Module 1: Parts of Speech

Nouns and pronouns, verbs and verb forms, adjectives and adverbs, prepositions, conjunctions, and interjections with practical examples.

Module 2: English Tenses

Present tenses, past tenses, future forms, perfect and continuous forms, and mixed tense practice for real-life application.

Module 3: Sentence Types & Structure

Simple sentences, compound sentences, complex sentences, relative clauses, passive voice, and conditional sentences.

Module 4: Advanced Grammar

Modal verbs, reported speech, gerunds and infinitives, articles and determiners, and advanced punctuation rules.

Identifying and correcting common grammar mistakes, subject-verb agreement, parallel structure, and modifier placement.

Module 6: Grammar in Context

Applying grammar rules in real-life speaking and writing situations, final assessment, and certification test.
